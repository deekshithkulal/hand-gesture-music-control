Hand Gesture Control

Learn the gestures you can use to control music playback in Gestonix.

Play

Open palm facing camera. Starts playback of the current song.

Pause

Closed fist. Pauses the currently playing song.

Volume Up

Index and middle finger up. Increases volume gradually.

Volume Down

Only index finger up. Lowers volume gradually.

Previous

Pinky finger out, thumb not extended. Skips to previous song.

Next

Index and pinky out, thumb folded. Skips to next song.

Voice Commands

  • Play – Say: "play"
  • Pause – Say: "pause" or "stop"
  • Next Song – Say: "next" or "skip"
  • Previous Song – Say: "previous" or "back"
  • Volume Up – Say: "volume up", "increase", "louder"
  • Volume Down – Say: "volume down", "decrease", "lower"

Tips for Accurate Detection

  • Keep your hand within 30–50 cm from the camera.
  • Ensure the background is well-lit but not too bright.
  • Perform gestures slowly at first to allow recognition.
  • Keep only one hand in the camera frame for best results.
  • Extend or fold fingers fully for precise detection.
